          Tab Management

          Configure auto-freezing and memory optimization features.

          Destroys a background tab's renderer process entirely after it's been inactive — not just backgrounded, actually gone — and rebuilds it (restoring the URL and scroll position) the instant you click back to it.

          The active tab is never frozen. Right-click any background tab to freeze it immediately instead of waiting. A frozen tab playing audio or mid-upload will have that killed along with everything else in it — that's inherent to actually destroying the process, not a bug.
