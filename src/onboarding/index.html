A browser built to help you finish things

Focus Browser puts real friction between you and distraction, runs focus sessions that protect themselves, and keeps everything strictly on your machine — no accounts, no sync.

Blocking that's actually hard to click through

Most blockers fail because turning them off takes one click. Here, getting past a blocked site costs a 60-second wait plus typing a phrase — enough friction that the impulse passes, but a genuine need still gets through.

Work sessions that lock in your focus automatically

Start a Pomodoro work session and site blocking plus Focus Mode switch on by themselves — then revert to exactly how you had them when the break begins. Set it up in Settings under Focus.

Your project files and tools, one click away

Open a local folder in the workspace sidebar, save Launch Profiles that open a set of tabs and apps together, and keep a bring-your-own-key AI chat panel beside your work.

See where your time actually goes

The Usage Dashboard tracks attention per site, locally only. Everything else lives in Settings — and the search box there finds any setting by name.
